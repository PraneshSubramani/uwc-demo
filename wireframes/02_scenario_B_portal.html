How it works

Your path to UWC in four steps

A clear, supported journey from your first question to the day you arrive on campus. Most applicants complete the form across 2-3 sittings.

1
Check eligibility
Three quick questions — your age, nationality, and country of residence — to make sure UWC is open to you for this cycle.
2
Create your account
Verify your email and set up your private applicant portal. You can save your progress and return any time before the deadline.
3
Complete your application
Six sections covering personal information, academic background, your personal statement, documents, guardian consent and declaration.
4
Submit & track
Submit before 30 November 2026. Your National Committee will guide you through eligibility review, interview and place allocation.
Why UWC

An education like no other

Since 1962 UWC has educated young people who go on to build understanding across the differences that divide our world.

🌍
Genuinely diverse
Every UWC class is selected to reflect the world — students from 80+ nationalities live, learn and disagree together for two transformative years.
💡
Academically transformative
The full IB Diploma combined with experiential learning — service, outdoor education, creativity — develops the whole person, not just the student.
🤝
Connected for life
UWC alumni are 65,000-strong across every continent — a network that becomes home, mentor and collaborator for the rest of your career.
