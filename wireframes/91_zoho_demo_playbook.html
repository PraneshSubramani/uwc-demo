The single-user demo strategy

Only one user account exists in this Zoho org
uwc_admin@a2zcloud.eu.com — that's you, Akanksha. No Clara, Paulo, Grace, or Helen user accounts. You demonstrate every role by:
  • Profile switching — Setup → Users → uwc_admin → change Profile dropdown → save → refresh → demo from that profile
  • Saved Views — pre-built “Clara's Queue”, “Paulo's Queue”, “Helen's Pending Pack” that filter the data exactly as that user would see it
  • Narration — “When Clara opens her CRM, this is exactly what she sees”
Profile switching takes 5 seconds. Always say “Let me switch to Clara's view” before clicking — never go silent during a switch.

Five rules for the panel

RuleWhy

Anticipated questions & answers

How long would it take to configure a new National Committee?Process
For a routine NC where the data model matches an existing one, configuration is a single record plus seeding volunteers and MoU obligations. Around two hours of admin time. For an NC that needs a localised application form — like the Brazil two-essay Local Questions structure — we'd add half a day for the form configuration and translation review.
How does this handle the 136 NC scale? Will the matrix performance hold?Scale
The Competency Matrix runs as a scheduled function nightly that's measured in seconds even at 200 bodies and 2,000 memberships. The NC Health Dashboard is similar. For real-time on-demand recompute we cap at 30 NCs per call to stay well within Zoho's API limits. We've stress-tested in a 1,800-user StudyIn deployment as a precedent.
What happens to the data if we move off Zoho in five years?Exit
Full data export is supported natively — CSV at the module level, JSON at the API level. Custom-module data is exportable on the same terms as standard. We typically scope a 30-day handover in the close-down phase to extract and verify. Your data is yours.
Can a National Committee override the global picklist for, say, Decline Reasons?Customisation
Picklists are global per field, but we can scope visibility — a Brazil-only reason can be hidden for Germany's profile. Where genuinely different vocabularies are needed we'd add a second field rather than fragment the same field; this preserves cross-NC reporting integrity.
If a reviewer leaves mid-cycle, what happens to their open scores?Continuity
The NC Admin reassigns the application to a new reviewer through the Assigned Reviewers field. The original reviewer's row remains on the audit trail. The system requires two completed reviews regardless of who completed them, so the count restarts at one and the new reviewer enters their scores. We've shipped that as a saved bulk action so an NC Admin can reassign multiple in one click.
How does the safeguarding flag interact with our existing safeguarding policy?Safeguarding
The CRM workflow is a routing and audit mechanism — it ensures concerns reach the right person within seconds and creates an unalterable record. The professional safeguarding response — contacting the applicant, working with school counsellors, involving local authorities where required — remains owned by your trained safeguarding leads. We'd integrate with your existing reporting standards and templates.

Reviewer training is in our delivery plan — one 60-minute live session plus a 5-minute video to reference. The interface is deliberately narrow for them: they see only what they need to score. Most reviewers we've trained are scoring confidently within ten minutes.

Zoho's EU data centre is multi-region within EU. Nightly backups are retained for 90 days as standard, longer on request. Recovery scripts

When the system stalls, broken behaviour appears, or a workflow doesn't fire as expected. Stay calm, narrate the design, move on.

Universal recovery posture

Three rules
  1. Never apologise more than once for the same issue. After the first apology, switch to narration.
  2. Don't show your console, your URL bar, or refresh repeatedly in front of the panel. If you need to fix something, switch your screen-share to a paused state and tell the panel: “Bear with me one moment.”
  3. Pranesh on Cliq is your fallback. If you're stuck for more than 30 seconds, message him “HELP” on Cliq. He'll reset on his side.

The two unrecoverable scenarios

If Zoho EU goes fully down for > 5 minutes
You have one option: open 99_data_model.html and 98_module_fields_catalog.html from your local copy. Walk the panel through the architecture and field model verbally. End with: “We're aware that data centre availability is an evaluation criterion for you. Zoho's published EU uptime is 99.99%; we'd be happy to share the SLA documentation as a follow-up.” Move to closing early. Don't extend.
If your laptop completely dies
Pranesh hands you a phone with the meeting on Zoho Meeting. You re-join. He takes over screen share from his laptop and continues. He has the same playbook open.

What good recovery looks like

The panel will not remember technical hiccups. They'll remember whether you stayed composed, whether you knew what should have happened, and whether you finished within time. A 5-second pause feels like 30 seconds — embrace it instead of filling it.
